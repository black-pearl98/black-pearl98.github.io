Campus Ambassador
WHAT TO DO AS A CAMPUS AMBASSADOR


The Campus Ambassador is accredited representative of SANDHAAN in his/her institute and is the cardinal link between the two.

Roles and Responsibilities:
1. Be the official ambassador and representative of MODEL CLUB B.I.T. Sindri in your college.
2. Active role in promotional, activities which includes displaying posters on notice boards, sharing e-posters, circulating e-mails and social media marketing.
3. Fetching participation from the college.

WHY SHOULD ONE BE A CAMPUS AMBASSADOR


1. Rewards and incentives:
-Certificates duly signed by the authorized officials of our institution.
-Gift vouchers and exciting goodies.


2. Learning opportunities:
Management skills are the real world skill required at this time of the era. Here, we provide you with an esteemed opportunity to experience the most exciting journey. It will help you to take lead and undertake responsibilities in order to gain an exposure of the significant communication and managerial skills.

WHO CAN APPLY


1. Anyone pursuing any degree but have relevant skills and interest.
2. Is currently in any year of graduation
